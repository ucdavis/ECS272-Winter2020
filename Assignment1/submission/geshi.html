LANGUAGES IN THE WORLD

ECS 272 InfoVis Assignment 1 By Ge Shi

Image source: AFTER BABYLON

The visualization shows 10 language families, spoken by at least 1% of world population, and the distributions in the world.

The visualization is interactive and people can click the buttons on the bottom to compare multiple languages.

The visualization is based on a 2D world map. The color of the map is light and the color for languages are more diverse and attractive. The design is intuitive and direct.

The visualization is good. The color choice helps people to tell different information and the interaction design supports inspection.
