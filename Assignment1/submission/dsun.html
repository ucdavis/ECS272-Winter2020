Paths to Congress

Image source: NY Times - About 5 percent of representatives don’t have a bachelor’s degree, compared with about two-thirds of Americans 25 and older.

1. What is being shown?

This visualization shows how the congressmen got to the current position.(Mainly their education backgrounds, career paths etc.)

2. Is the visualization interactive or static? If interactive, how can it be interacted with?

It's interactive(Please check out the original site for more). It has a drop down list where you could select one specific individual to see his/her path.

3. What are some of the design choices and visualization techniques used?

It uses two different colors to mark the people from Democratic or Republican party respectively, and it splits the chart into different phases to distinguish different stages of their paths.

4. What, in your opinion, is the rationale behind the design choices?

Colors for different group of people, lines to simulate the life paths and directions, etc.

5. Do you think this is a good visualization? Give a short critique on why or why not.

Yes. From the chart we can easily see the different path patterns, the similarity and differences among the current congressmen. Also we can check out one single congressman to see his/her path.
